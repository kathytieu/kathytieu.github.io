About us

At our prior company, our logs scaled rapidly.

We didn't want to spend additional tens of thousands of dollars per month on traditional logging tools. So we threw away logs more than a few hours old, which made debugging super painful.

That's why we built Scanner. It should be possible to store logs at massive scale with incredible cost efficiency, without sacrificing speed.
